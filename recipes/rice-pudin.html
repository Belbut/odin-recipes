Rice Pudin

Description

This is a simple and fast way to enjoy rice pudding.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 424 calories; protein 10.6g; carbohydrates 76.2g; fat 8.9g; cholesterol 32.9mg; sodium 121.5mg.

Ingredients

    2 cups cooked white rice 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk Â½ cup white sugar

Steps

    Combine the rice, cinnamon, milk, and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir occasionally and bring mixture to a boil; Cover and reduce heat to low. Cook until thick and creamy, 5 to 7 minutes.
